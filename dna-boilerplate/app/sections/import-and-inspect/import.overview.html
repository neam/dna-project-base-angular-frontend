Import sessions

The first step into getting accounting done is to import whatever you already have in front of you or near at hand.

  Upload exported bank and credit card data files

  Upload or take photos of receipts, travel tickets, invoices etc

  Dig through your mailbox after receipts, travel tickets, invoices and the likes

  Import accounting and/or invoice data directly from your accounting and/or invoicing software

Before doing the above:

  • Login to your internet banking service, export transaction information from all your accounts that may be relevant for accounting purposes.
